About Safari Insider

“I believe that a true safari isn’t something you just see , it’s something you feel. It’s the smell of the rain on the red dust of Tsavo, the rhythm of the Great Migration in the Mara, and the warmth of a Kenyan smile that makes you feel like you’ve finally come home.”

Who I Am

My name is Rachel Chebet, and I am the heart behind Safari Insider.

I didn’t just start this brand to book hotels; I started it to share the soul of my country with the world. While I have spent years studying the intricate logistics of tourism and the science of hospitality, my true education came from growing up in the beauty of Kenya.

I am a local who knows the “secret” spots that don’t make it into the brochures,the quiet viewpoints, the most authentic cultural encounters, and the boutique camps where the land is respected.

My Promise to You

When you plan a trip with me, you aren’t just another booking in a system. You are a guest in my home. I bridge the gap between your high expectations for comfort and safety and the raw, unedited beauty of the African wild.

Whether it is navigating the 2026 ETA process or finding the perfect sundowner spot overlooking the Rift Valley, I handle the details so you can focus on the wonder.

The Invitation — Your Journey

Planning a trip to Africa is a milestone,a dream that deserves care. I would love to walk this journey with you.

From our first discovery call to the moment you board your flight home, I will be your “insider” on the ground, ensuring every moment is as magical as you imagined.
