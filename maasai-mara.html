The Iconic Migration: Maasai Mara

7 Days • Luxury • The Great Migration Experience

The Vibe

The ultimate bucket list adventure. High-action wildlife encounters, luxury tented camps, and the raw magic of the Great Migration across the golden plains of the Maasai Mara.

Day 1: Welcome to the City in the Sun

  • Arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.
  • Private transfer to a boutique hotel in Karen.
  • Insider Activity: Afternoon visit to the Giraffe Center to hand feed endangered Rothschild giraffes.

Day 2: Flight to the Wild

  • Breakfast and transfer to Wilson Airport.
  • Scenic flight to the Maasai Mara.
  • Landing on a bush airstrip with your private 4x4 safari vehicle.
  • Afternoon game drive followed by classic Bush Sundowners.

Day 3 – 5: The Great Migration Experience

  • Three full days of exploration across the Maasai Mara.
  • Witness dramatic river crossings (seasonal) and encounters with the Big Five.
The Highlight: Sunrise Hot Air Balloon Safari over the Mara River, followed by a champagne breakfast in the wild.
Insider Tip: Visit a private Maasai village for an authentic cultural experience with the guardians of the Mara.

Day 6: One Last Roar

  • Final early morning game drive.
  • Mid morning flight back to Nairobi.
  • Farewell dinner at a traditional Kenyan restaurant.

Day 7: Journey Home

  • Relaxed morning.
  • Last minute souvenir shopping at the Kazuri Beads factory.
  • Transfer to the airport for international departure.
