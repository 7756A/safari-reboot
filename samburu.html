The Northern Frontier: Hidden Gems

8 Days • Samburu & Mount Ololokwe

The Vibe

Off the beaten path for the traveler who wants to avoid the crowds.

Days 1 – 3: Samburu National Reserve

  • Discover the "Samburu Special Five" (Grevy’s Zebra, Reticulated Giraffe, etc.).
  • Insider Tip: Meet the Samburu warriors and learn the art of tracking in the semi-arid north.

Days 4 – 6: Ol Pejeta Conservancy

  • Meet the world's last two Northern White Rhinos.
  • Focus on conservation and night game drives you can't do in the National Parks.

Days 7 – 8: Mount Kenya & Departure

  • Relax in the lush forests of the mountain foothills before heading back to Nairobi.
